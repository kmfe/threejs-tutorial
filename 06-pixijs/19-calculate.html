Upstream

Training focus

  • Short-term visual memory
  • Concentration
  • Executive functions
  • Reaction speed

Principles of the training

  • The Upstream game trains multiple components of attention. Changes in the instructions demand switching attention and concentration of attention.
  • At the same time, the division of attention between several elements is trained.

How to play this game

The goal of the game is to determine the direction and the movement of each leaf.

If the leaf is blue, press the arrow corresponding to the orientation of the leaf.

If the leaf is orange, press the arrow corresponding to the movement of the leaf.

If the leaf is brown, press the arrow corresponding to the rule of the previous leaf.
